Projects

In this page I present a selection of significant projects that I have completed during my academic and personal journey. Each project represents an opportunity for growth, where I have been able to apply the knowledge acquired, develop new skills and face concrete challenges. Through these experiences, I have learned the importance of teamwork, creativity and perseverance.

"FOOD & FASHION IN 3D"

      from 11-07-2022 to 17-04-2024

The "FOOD & FASHION IN 3D" project blends technology, fashion, and food design. Students created accessories inspired by culinary elements using 3D printers, exploring digital design, sustainability, and creativity.

"MAB FOOD ART" WORKSHOP FUTURA SCHOOL-CASERTA"

      from 12-04-2024 to 15-04-2024

Held at the Royal Palace of Caserta for Scuola Futura, the "MAB FOOD ART" workshop challenged students to create a new pizza representing local identity using traditional ingredients. I participated in the FOOD ART team.

"FOCUS ON CERTIFICATIONS"

      from 06-02-2024 to 07-05-2024

This Cambridge-led course prepared us for the PET (B1) English exam, enhancing our grammar, vocabulary, and conversation skills for international certification.

"CISCO IT ESSENTIALS"

      from 20-09-2023 to 23-05-2024

The Cisco IT Essentials course introduced us to computer hardware, software, and networking fundamentals—essential skills for future IT paths.

"DIGITAL INNOVATION AWARD"

      2023/2024

"GoMino", developed with Exprivia and HRCOFFEE, uses AI and robotics to support cognitive growth in kids (ages 3–7). The project won a national award for educational innovation.
